FAQ

Common Questions

How long does the process take?

Manuscript Prep takes 1-2 weeks. Author Launch takes 2-3 weeks. Complete Publishing takes 3-4 weeks. Sermon-to-Book typically takes 3-4 weeks depending on the number of messages.

Do I keep the rights to my book?

Absolutely. You own 100% of your manuscript, your cover, and all rights. KDP is published under your account, and royalties go directly to you.

For Sermon-to-Book, what kind of YouTube videos work?

Any sermon, teaching, or message you've preached works as long as the audio is clear. We need 4-10 messages on a related theme to build a coherent book. Conference series, sermon series, or Sunday messages on a topic are all great starting points.

What if I don't have a finished manuscript?

If you have a draft, we can work with it. If you're starting from scratch, the Sermon-to-Book service is the easiest path — we build the book from your existing preached content.

How are royalties handled?

All royalties go directly to you through your own KDP account. We do not take a percentage of your book sales.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes. For packages over $1,000, we can arrange a 50% upfront, 50% on completion payment plan. Contact us before purchasing.
